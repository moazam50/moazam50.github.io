Snort vs. Suricata IDS Comparison

Technologies Used: Snort, Suricata, Traffic Generators, Wireshark, Virtual Machines

This project involved a detailed comparison between Snort and Suricata to evaluate their performance, detection capabilities, and resource usage under different attack scenarios. The goal was to determine which IDS performs better in real-world network environments.

  • Detection Accuracy: Both systems detected known attacks effectively, with Suricata excelling in complex environments.
  • Performance: Suricata showed better scalability and handled high-speed traffic more effectively than Snort.
  • Ease of Use: Snort was easier to configure for basic setups, while Suricata required more advanced configuration for optimal performance.
  • False Positives/Negatives: Suricata had fewer false negatives, making it more reliable for complex attacks.
  • Scalability: Suricata demonstrated superior scalability, especially in large, high-traffic networks.

Conclusion: Suricata is better suited for large-scale, high-traffic environments, while Snort is effective for smaller, less complex networks.
